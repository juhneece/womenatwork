About

Women at Work was founded to connect ambitious and brilliant women to learn from each other’s experiences. Our community believes the knowledge and advice we share will help us become the best versions of ourselves in our professional and personal lives.

Each day is an opportunity to take at least one action that moves you forward. Whether it’s speaking up in a meeting, sending a bold email, or negotiating your salary - our members are bias to action. Because even the smallest actions can change things for the better.
